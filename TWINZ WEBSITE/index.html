Revolutionizing Transportation with eVTOL Technology

Twynz Aerospace Ltd. is an emerging aerospace company dedicated to revolutionizing air travel with advanced electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft. Based in London, we focus on delivering sustainable and efficient air transport solutions that meet the demands of modern aviation. In addition to our eVTOL innovations, we provide expert mechanical design consulting across aerospace, automotive, robotics, and healthcare industries, ensuring that we contribute to a greener future across various sectors.
